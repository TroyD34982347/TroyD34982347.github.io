Open Chatting Zone:

Designed for casual conversations with friends. it is less secluded, and it is more calm and open.

New Friends Zone:

A quieter area for meeting new people, creating an inviting environment. It is soft, secluded, and calming, meant to meet new friends.

Quiet Space:

A calm area for solitude and unwinding, where you can relax, and think, and brainstorm.

Private Rooms:

Noise-cancelled rooms for private discussions, making it a safe space for venting if needed. They are able to contain up to 4 people each, or 5 for certain cases.

Food Section:

A dedicated area for food that also teaches basic cooking skills, with accommodations for dietary restrictions (e.g., no beef, pork, etc.). It has full ventilation, and is sealed off from the rest of the building so scents do not escape.

Features:

Accessibility: The center is fully wheelchair and other mental or physical disability accessible with ramps, flat floors, and wide open aisles throughout, so that people do not accidentally bump into each other.

Ambience: The building features dim lighting and non-fluorescent lights to make comfort, particularly for individuals with migraines, anxiety, or paranoia. The overall colour scheme uses dark, cozy tones to create a warm atmosphere, such as black, dark brown, deep emerald, etc.

Plush Couches arranged around communal tables with board games available in one section, promoting relaxation and social interaction. Tables and chairs are set up throughout the space to encourage new friendships and conversations.

Physical Space Description: The building is a large, quiet community center designed for open chatting and interaction. It has a spacious, non-crowded layout with clearly defined zones to cater to various social needs. The overall design is classic and cozy, promoting a sense of comfort and belonging for all visitors.

Activities:

There are lots of amazing books to read everywhere.

We have many fun and thrilling boardgames in the open chatting zone and the new friend's zone.

You can chat with anyone you want, as long as they are willing to.

More Features:

There is even more features and safe-guards to keep you feeling happy and safe.

Privacy and Safety:

Photography and video recording are strictly prohibited in non-private areas to ensure privacy, while free therapist rooms are available for those who need mental health support.

Scent-Free Space:

The center prioritizes a scent-free environment for the comfort of all guests.

Environment Quality:

The building maintains a comfortable room temperature that is neither humid nor dry, with excellent ventilation throughout.
